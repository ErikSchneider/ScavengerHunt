How to play

  1. Get some friends, separate into teams and choose someone to be the game-leader.
  2. The game-leader creates a new game and shares the lobby code with the other teams can join. (only one phone per team is needed).
  3. When all teams are ready the game-leader starts the game
  4. Figure out where the clues referencing and get there as fast as possible and submit your location to see if you are right.
  5. The fastest team to solve all five clues or has solved the most before the timer runs out wins!

Tips on playing

  1. Think outside the box! The clues are tough!
  2. Think about the route you'll take. Solving one at a time may be easy, but it could take you the long way. Plan your route!
  3. Research online, ask locals, use whatever resources you have to solve the clues.
  4. Keep an eye out for white markers in the real world they usually are where you need to be to solve the clue.
